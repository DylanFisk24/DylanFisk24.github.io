Aspiring Twitch streamer and video game speedrunner, known for speedrunning games like A Hat in Time, Sonic Adventure 2: Battle, and The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, among others.

Speedrunning

Over the past five years, one of my most prominent preoccupations has been speedrunning some of my favorite video games. What began as simply a means of passing the abundance of free time acquired from the COVID-19 pandemic soon became a long-standing passion of mine. As I continued lowering my times for each game, I fell in love with the continual pushing of my limits as I strived for excellence. As of now, I have uploaded speedruns for nine video games. These games are listed below in the order I've run them, along with my current PBs for each:

  1. A Hat in Time - 37m 10s 700ms (Any%, Lag Abuse)
  2. Sonic Adventure 2: Battle - 27m 24s 580ms (Hero Story)
  3. Shantae: Half-Genie Hero - 1h 07m 40s (Any%, Glitchless)
  4. Shantae and the Pirate's Curse - 45m 27s (Any%, Pirate Mode)
  5. Sonic Heroes - 42m 55s (Team Rose, LTS)
  6. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild - 30m 51s (Any%)
  7. Here Comes Niko! - 20m 26s 410ms (Any%)
  8. Below the Ocean - 18m 47s 267ms - (Any%, All Chapters)
  9. Celeste 64 - 17s 233ms (Any%)
  10. HASTE: Broken Worlds - 6m 32s (Demo Glitchless)

Twitch Streaming

My career as a live streamer on twitch.tv stemmed naturally from my newfound passion for speedrunning. As I learned the runs and improved my times, I was excited to share this process with others, and live streaming became the avenue through which I could both document and share my progress. Ever since, streaming has helped me connect with new communities, find new friends, and expand my internet presence. Some of my accomplishments include:

  • Reaching affiliate status on Twitch, allowing monetization of my content
  • Surpassing 150 followers
  • Building a small community through Discord, housing over 40 members

I hope to see you all in my stream chat real soon!
