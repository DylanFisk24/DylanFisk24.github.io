Speedrunning

Over the past five years, one of my most prominent preoccupations has been speedrunning some of my favorite video games. What began as simply a means of passing the abundance of free time acquired from the COVID-19 pandemic soon became a long-standing passion of mine. As I continued lowering my times for each game, I fell in love with the continual pushing of my limits as I strived for excellence. As of now, I have uploaded speedruns for nine video games. These games are listed below in the order I've run them, along with my current PBs for each:

  1. A Hat in Time - 37:10.700 (Any%, Lag Abuse)
  2. Sonic Adventure 2: Battle - 27:24.580 (Hero Story)
  3. Shantae: Half-Genie Hero - 1:07:40.000 (Any%, Glitchless)
  4. Shantae and the Pirate's Curse - 45:27.000 (Any%, Pirate Mode)
  5. Sonic Heroes - 42:55.000 (Team Rose, LTS)
  6. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild - 30:51.000 (Any%)
  7. Here Comes Niko! - 20:26.410 (Any%)
  8. Below the Ocean - 18:47.267 - (Any%, All Chapters)
  9. Celeste 64 - 00:17.233 (Any%)

Twitch Streaming

My career as a live streamer on twitch.tv stemmed naturally from my newfound passion for speedrunning. As I learned the runs and improved my times, I was excited to share this process with others, and live streaming became the avenune through which I could both document and share my progress. Ever since, streaming has helped me connect with new communities, find new friends, and expand my internet presence. Some of my accomplishments include:

  • Reaching affiliate status on Twitch, allowing monetization of my content
  • Surpassing 150 followers
  • Building a small community through Discord, housing over 40 members
